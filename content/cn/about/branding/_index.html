UICHCC 徽标

UICHCC 徽标。 允许的最小数字版本大小：25 个像素（高度）

UICHCC 徽标是我们俱乐部身份的重要表现。应用于形象传达时，绝不应该扭曲或不按规则进行派生。由于徽标是一种可识别且高度可见的品牌资产，因此始终如一地应用徽标至关重要。

The design of this logo is inspired by Nagasaki Prefectural Art Museum's visual design by Kenya Hara and the patten's idea comes from Apple Ⅰ's circuit board.

Solid color block beneath

When placing the UICHCC logo above a solid color block, you have to make sure that the width and the height of the logo isn't larger than 85% of the block.

UICHCC logo with black & white background, border for showing the edge. Minimum digital size: 150 pixels (height)

Color

Correct Usage:

The UICHCC logo is primarily used in black and white. It can sit on top of opposite color background.

Incorrect Usage:

The UICHCC logo should never be used in white on top of any light colors. Additionally it shouldn’t be used with our dark colors, neither coloring it with a dark color, or using our primary black over a dark background.
